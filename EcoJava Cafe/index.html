Ecojava's Philosophy

If anything truly defines us, it would be our outlook on brewing the best coffee and providing the best customer service. We only purchase locally grown, organic ingredients; we promote fair trade, and service nothing short of premium. Ecojava sincerely hopes our efforts make your visits as enjoyable and relaxed as possible.

Cafe Recommended

Guatemala Justicia Brew


Guatemala Justicia – Grown by the indigenous people of Guatemala in the highlands near Atitlan on lands recovered through agrarian reform since 1998. This coffee is grown using only organic principles and produces one of the most aromatic and sweet cups known to Guatemala.

Cake Crumble Scone


Our bakery's long-standing favourite. With organic ingredients, we bake a scone and stuff it with cinnamon crumble – finishing off with light icing drizzle. This treat makes for a wonderful breakfast!

Lavender Lemon Blueberry Biscuits


This treat comes with your choice of icecream and serves well as a dessert! We would strongly suggest those with a sweet tooth try this, as there's no holdbacks on the sugary sweetness. Enjoy.
