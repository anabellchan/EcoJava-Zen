Ecojava Cafe Menu

Beverages
From a peaceful morning mild to a rush of excitement bold, each of our exceptional beverages is made from the best Fairtrade Certified Organic ingredients. Artisan roasted in Vancouver, Canada, we ensure every cup is just right – so that you’ll love it with the same passion that went in to creating it.
Baked Goods
Our gourmet-baked goods taste so yummy that you won't believe they're all gluten free... But they are! Our special baking mix always results in the finest, freshest confections... Ideal for those seeking nutritious alternateives to as wheat-based diet, with no compromises in taste or texture.
Sandwiches
We understand a really good sandwich. It’s about great, freshly baked bread. It’s about generous helpings of fresh, juicy ingredients. It’s about slow cooking and fast chopping, the soft embrace of pillowy bread around crunchy pickles and sumptuous deli.
Desserts
Our desserts are baked fresh daily and contain no additives or artificial flavourings. Only the finest ingredients including 100% Canadian butter and free-range eggs are used. The moist, rich buttercream frostings will melt in your mouth.
Coffee Beans
We offer an ample variety of coffee, from Fair Trade to Rainforest, Specialty to Direct Trade, as crops come and go, we may add or subtract, but we always work on providing you options which suit your taste. If you are looking for something and its not here, please send us a message and we can see if we can get what you need for you.
