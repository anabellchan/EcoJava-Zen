Coffee Beans

Espresso Roast

Intense, caramelly sweet and perfect with steamed milk. Delicious as brewed coffee too.

How to make a great coffee

  1. Use cold water.
  2. Grind beans right before brewing takes place
  3. Measure 3 tbsp (26 g) ground coffee for every 9 fl oz (250 ml) water.

Breakfast Blend

A lively and lighter roast with a crisp finish.

How to make a great coffee

  1. Use warm, filtered water and a clean machine.
  2. Grind beans just before brewing.
  3. Measure 2 tbsp (14 g) ground coffee for every 8 fl oz (220 ml) water.

Sumatra

Full-bodied with a smooth mouthfeel and lingering herbal flavours. Amazing with savoury foods.

How to make a great coffee

  1. Use cold, filtered water and a clean machine.
  2. Grind beans just before brewing.
  3. Measure 2 tbsp (10 g) ground coffee for every 6 fl oz (180 ml) water.
